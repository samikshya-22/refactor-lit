LIT- An Amalgamation of Academics and Research Based Learning

LIT, Bhubaneswar formerly known as Lakshya Institute Of Technology is a beacon of innovation and excellence in technical education. With a mission to shape the leaders of tomorrow, LIT offers a transformative learning experience that blends academic rigor with real-world relevance. Our cutting-edge curriculum, designed in collaboration with industry experts, ensures that our graduates are equipped with the skills and knowledge to thrive in today's fast-paced technological landscape. LIT established in 2016, sets the benchmarks of education with a systematic approach to match the best of practices in IT standards in India, having a clear-cut objective to train & upgrade thousands of students for different programs.

LIT offers three year undergraduate programmes such as BCA, BSC (ITM), BSC (CS), BSC (Data Science), taught by our professors with decades of experience and technical acumen. It has been a dream institution of the Founder-Chairman Prof. Susant K. Rout. The potential of Odisha's IT skilled manpower and its demands in the IT industry and for re-emergence of an authentic institution. Prof. Rout has given his heart and soul to the institute i.e. LIT. The college is an endeavour with a practical approach to achieve the target objective of creation of quality human resources. At LIT, we believe in holistic development, nurturing not just the intellect but also the character of our students.
Our alumni, now trailblazers in various industries, exemplify the impact of an LIT education. Join us at LIT, where we turn potential into achievement and dreams into reality.

LIT CAMPUS

LIT is home to many national and international students. The campus boasts of modern marvel infrastructure with every construction equipped with the modern facilities.LIT is a peaceful paradise, far away from the noise & bustle of an urban area. The LIT campus is a high-tech oriented territory at par with the world's leading global educational institutions.

LIT is the perfect choice of students in terms of studies and environment. It is strategically the perfect fit for the students who want to pursue world class education in India. In addition to excellent academics, LIT also offers the students an abundant scope of recreational and outdoor activities to undergo overall development.
